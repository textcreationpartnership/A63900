#####Front#####
AN ARGUMENT IN DEFENCE OF THE HOSPITALLER OF St. THOMAS SOUTHWARK AND OF His Fellow-Servants and Fri
1. To the Right Honourable The LORD-MAYOR of the City of London. and to the Honourable the Court of Aldermen.

#####Body#####

1. THE CASE Between Mr. Hughes, the late pretended Hoſpitaller of St. Thomas Southwark, and Mr. Turner the preſent Lawfull Incumbent of the ſame, truly Stated.

    _ The Caſe as it was Stated by Mr S. and was reſolved by thoſe Learned Gentlemen, is as follows.

    _ Mr. Pollexfen's Opinion of the ſame Caſe.

    _ Sir John Holt's Opinion to the ſame purpoſe.
The Firſt obſervation I ſhall make from hence ſhall be taken from the ſtateing of the Caſe it ſelf, 
    _ POSTSCRIPT.
